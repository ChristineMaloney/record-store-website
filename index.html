History of Record Store Day!

Record Store Day started in 2007. Stores partake in RSD on all continets except for Antartica!

All RSD participants are independent stores! Yep! That's right, ALL!

All Stores that participate are independently owned. That means no big corporations! Check out the list of stores on the RSD website!

Why Record Stores?~Quote~

"When I was old enough to work, I got a job for a record store handing out flyers to promote the store. I used to walk around bored out of my mind until lunch time when I would read all the record sleeves. I would finish work for the day and blow all the money I made on CDs at the very same shop that just paid me! I can't seem to walk past a record store without going in. The more independent the better, I usually fall in love with the music that is blasting as I walk in and always leave with a few rare items I have been searching for. You can't beat the atmosphere of an indie record store, it's the best!" - Michael Chislett (The Academy Is…)
